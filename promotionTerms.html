Terms

Grantees will be selected at random from the pool of qualified applicants by April 31st, 2015 and grants will be shipped shortly thereafter. To qualify, applicants must be full-time classroom instructors teaching a STEM subject in a secondary classroom and must complete a full video observation cycle on the Ask A Mentor platform by April 15th, 2015. A full video observation cycle includes a video of a classroom lesson with specific questions around practice, a response from the mentor, and a follow up video or reflection based on the implementation of mentor feedback. Retail value of the iPad mini 3TM grant is $499. No payment necessary to be included in the grant opportunity.
